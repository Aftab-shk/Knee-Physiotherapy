Four steps to safer recovery

From X-ray to guided exercise in minutes. Every step is informed by your specific imaging and surgery history.

1
🩻
Upload your X-ray

Photograph or scan your knee X-ray and upload it. Tell us which knee — left, right, or both.

2
📋
Enter your surgery details

Select your procedure type — ACL reconstruction, TKR, meniscus repair, arthroscopy, or OA management — and how many weeks it's been.

3
🎯
Get your personalised plan

AI grades your X-ray (KL Grade 0–4) and combines it with your recovery stage to prescribe the right exercises — with safe angle limits for each.

4
📹
Exercise with live oversight

The webcam tracks your knee angle in real time. Go past your limit and the screen turns red with an audible alarm — instantly.

What it does

Built on clinical logic, not guesswork

Every feature in PhysioAI is grounded in a real clinical rule. Nothing is invented.

🧠
AI KL Grade Classification

EfficientNet-B4 trained on 12,000+ knee X-rays classifies your joint as KL Grade 0–4. Includes CLAHE preprocessing for X-ray contrast enhancement.

Surgery-Aware Protocols

16 distinct exercise sets across ACL, TKR, meniscus repair, and arthroscopy — each phase gated by weeks since surgery, not by imaging alone.

The Two-Input Architecture

The X-ray sets the ceiling. Your surgery stage selects the exercises. Neither input alone is enough. This is what separates PhysioAI from every other tool.

Core clinical innovation

Real-Time Safety Lock

MediaPipe BlazePose tracks hip, knee, and ankle landmarks at 24+ fps. The safety lock fires in under 100ms — red screen and audio alarm — when any limit is crossed.

Sub-100ms latency

Runs Entirely On-Device

No patient data is sent to any server after setup. Inference, angle tracking, and session storage all run locally. Safe for clinic deployment behind a firewall.

No cloud dependency
