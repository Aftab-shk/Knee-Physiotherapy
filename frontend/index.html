Four steps to safer recovery

From X-ray to guided exercise in minutes. Every step is informed by your specific imaging and surgery history.

1
🩻
Upload your X-ray

Photograph or scan your knee X-ray and upload it. Tell us which knee — left, right, or both.

2
📋
Enter your surgery details

Select your procedure type — ACL reconstruction, TKR, meniscus repair, arthroscopy, or OA management — and how many weeks it's been.

3
🎯
Get your personalised plan

AI grades your X-ray (KL Grade 0–4) and combines it with your recovery stage to prescribe the right exercises — with safe angle limits for each.

4
📹
Exercise with live oversight

The webcam tracks your knee angle in real time. Go past your limit and the screen turns red with an audible alarm — instantly.

🔴

The moment you exceed the safe limit —
your screen turns red and the alarm sounds.

Every exercise has its own angle limit — derived from your X-ray severity and your recovery protocol. The webcam tracker enforces it in real time at under 100ms latency. No delay. No guessing. No re-injury from going a degree too far.
